Back Alley Gin Rules

Objective

Accumulate the highest points at the end of the 25 hands to win.

The Deal

The person who selects the highest card from the deck deals first. For the first hand each player is dealt 1 card, increase each hand by 1 card until you reach a hand of 13 cards. Then decrease 1 card per hand until you are back to 1 card.

Trump Cards

Spades are always Trump. The Big Joker (BJ) is the highest trump card followed by the Small Joker (SJ), then the Ace, King, etc.

Following Suit

You must always follow the lead suit. If you are void in that suit, it’s the perfect opportunity to play a trump card.

Bidding

Calculate the tricks (hands) you think you can make based on the strength of your hand.

Scoring

Each bid is worth 4 points. If you exceed your bid, add 1 point for every trick made over your bid. If you miss your bid – deduct 4 points per bid.

CPU Personalities

CPU players can be toggled between "Normal" and "Aggressive" in the settings. Aggressive bots bid more frequently and play high trump cards earlier.
